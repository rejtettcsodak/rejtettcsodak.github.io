Short story

I've been interested in programming since my childhood, at first I only used scratch, and later I switched to Unity. It was a bit of a leap, but I didn't regret it in the end. In addition to programming, I do graphic design, edit videos, do animations, design UI & UX, and I really like to organize big projects.

Some of my programs

  • Koller

    The plan is to create an advanced, automated, digital student hostel system. There are currently 7 of us working on it. It would digitalize the content of the students booklet, the teachers diary, gate exits and entrances, and all the other data that is necessary for the harmonious operation of the student hostel. It will have a clean mobile and web interface for both students and teachers.

    Koller page

  • Undercave

    This is was one of my main project before Koller and also my dream game. I don't have much to say about it, it's a 3D survival game that takes place underground in unknown depths. If you're interested, join my Discord server and subscribe to my dev channel.

  • Berek Zoltán és a Legendás Tömlőssajt

    (or Berek Zoltán and the Legendary Tömlőssajt in English. Tömlőssajt is a processed cheese type that probably doesn't exist in your country)

    In this game, you take on the role of a YouTuber, Zoltán Berek, with whom you have to save your viewers, as an evil brushcutter wants to destroy them because you no longer make brushcutter videos. However, the Giant Brushcutter is not alone. He has revived many other brushcutter to prevent you from your rescue mission.

    Berek Zoltán és a Legendás Tömlőssajt | Sztori Mód Release Trailer

    This is my first relatively big game, so don't expect much from it. (The game promises updates, but I don't.)

    This game was made for the 2022 GMTK Game Jam. It's a short (and buggy) strategy game that I made in two days. This was my first Game Jam so don't expect much from it.

  • Puskás 12.B campaign and student day: Jesus

    For the third time, the class that defeated us twice had already left school, so we had a slightly better chance.

    We had a lot of ideas from previous years, so this year we had to vote, and Jesus won in the end. It was difficult because we were also graduating, but we tried to come up with as many funny campaign videos, posters and programs as possible. In the end, we won big. This was where the new things started, because until then, campaigning had always been the end of the road for us. This year, for some reason, we had very little time to organize and it caused a lot of difficulties. There were times when I was awake until dawn, even though I had to get up very early the next day to prepare everything at school.

    In the end, we got the feedback that almost everyone enjoyed the day. I would also like to thank all my classmates who took part in the organization.
